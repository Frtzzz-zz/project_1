What is Earth made of?

The world is comprised of many things

The crust is made up of:

  • Oxygen
  • Silicon
  • Aluminum
  • Iron
  • Calcium
  • Sodium
  • Potassium
  • Magnesium

The mantle is made mostly of rocks or silicates such as:

  • Silicon
  • Magnesium
  • Aluminum
  • Silicon

The planet's core, both inner and outer, is composed of:

  • Iron
  • Nickel
  • Silicon
  • Other heavy metals

What is going on in Earth?

The Earth has gone through many, many revolutions. Birth, Growth, Evolution, Civilization, Deprecation, Resurrection and Rebirth. As time passed, it grew more and more along with its organisms. And as such, consciousness existed to a point where things are made from our own human hands

One skill branched off to another. Shared across generations. Practices, Cultures, Experiences, all passed down through stories in order to preserve them.
